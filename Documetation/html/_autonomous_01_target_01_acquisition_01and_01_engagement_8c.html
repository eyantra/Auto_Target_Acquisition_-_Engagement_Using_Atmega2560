SIGNAL ( SIG_USART1_RECV  )

Function called upon recieving a character on the serial port ie UART1.This Function analyses the recieved character and takes appropriate action.

ASCII-Code Action Taken
1 Move the bat back for 500ms
0x30 Moves both the servo Motors to upper_limit
0x31-0x39 Moves camera according to the nine squares method using the servo motor 2 and moving the bot left or right.Here the rotation is of rotation_angle and in the case of Movement of the bot for the time left_right_delay
0x35 Beeps the buzzer and fires the laser for a short amount of time which is in servo_3.
>0x39 This moves the servo moter 2 to an angle =(ascii_code-0x40)*rotation_angle
